To create a Steam account, visit the Steam website and click on "Login" at the top right corner, then select "Join Steam" and follow the on-screen instructions to sign up.

To purchase a game, log in to your Steam account, browse or search for the game you want, click "Add to Cart," and follow the checkout process to complete your purchase.

Steam accepts various payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, Steam Wallet, and other regional payment options.

To request a refund, go to the Steam Support page, select the game or product, and follow the instructions for requesting a refund. Refunds are typically granted if the request is made within 14 days of purchase and the game has less than 2 hours of playtime.
